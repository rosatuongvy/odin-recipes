Skillet Lasagna

Description

This no-bake skillet lasagna is made right on your stovetop and is a fast and easy alternative to store-bought hamburger mixes!

Ingredients

  • ½ pound ground beef
  • ½ (28 ounce) jar spaghetti sauce
  • 1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes
  • ½ onion, chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 cups dried malfada noodles
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Step 1: Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook and stir beef in the hot skillet until browned and crumbly, 5 to 7 minutes. Drain and discard grease. Add spaghetti sauce, tomatoes, onion, garlic, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Cook over low heat until sauce is hot, about 15 minutes.
  2. Step 2: Meanwhile, fill a large pot with lightly salted water and bring to a rolling boil. Cook mafalda noodles at a boil until tender yet firm to the bite, about 8 minutes. Drain.
  3. Step 3: Add cooked and drained noodles to the sauce and stir until completely coated. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese on top.
  4. Step 4: Set an oven rack about 6 inches from the heat source and preheat the oven's broiler.
  5. Step 5: Place skillet under the hot broil and cook until cheese is golden and bubbly, 3 to 5 minutes.
